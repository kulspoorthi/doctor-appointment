ABOUT US






Dr.Phaniraj G.

MBBS,D.Ortho,McH.(Ortho)






Dr.Phaniraj G. is a senior orthopaedic consulting surgeon with an experience of 25 plus years. He has completed his MBBS, D. Ortho from MR Medical College Gulbarga. Also, he holds a post graduate degree in McH in Orthopaedics from Mumbai.
Before establishing Mukund Orthopaedic center, Dr.Phaniraj G began his career as a doctor at Gulbarga. Later he has served and worked as a visiting doctor at various hospitals of Gulbarga, Humnabad, Hospet, Raichur, Guntakal and Bellary. Currently, Dr.Phaniraj G. is the founder and senior consultant at Mukund Orthopaedic Center. He specialises in General Orthopaedics, Trauma ,Bone and Joint Diseases, Rheumatology, Paediatric Rheumatology, Peripheral Neurology, Angiology/Vascular Medicine and Connective Tissue Disease along with these Mukund Orthopaedic Center -A day care centre has X-Ray, Physiotherapy and Corrective appliances.
